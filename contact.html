Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started as a developer?
Sign up for a free account, complete your profile with your skills, and start browsing available projects. You can apply to projects that match your skills.
How do I post a project as a company?
Create a company account, post your project requirements, and receive applications from qualified student developers within hours.
How do payments work?
Payments are processed securely through our platform. Funds are held in escrow until project completion, ensuring safety for both parties.
What is the premium subscription?
Premium subscription (₹69/month) gives developers access to full project details, contact information, and unlimited applications.

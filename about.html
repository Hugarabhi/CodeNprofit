About codenprofit

India's first Business-to-Student (B2S) platform connecting companies with talented student developers

Building the future of student development

codenprofit was born from a simple idea: talented student developers deserve real-world project experience while earning money, and businesses deserve access to fresh, innovative talent at affordable rates.

What started as a small community project has grown into India's premier B2S platform, helping hundreds of students launch their careers and dozens of businesses bring their ideas to life.

We believe in the power of student innovation and the value of practical experience. Our platform bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Our Mission

To empower student developers with real-world project experience and fair compensation while helping businesses access high-quality development talent at affordable rates. We're building a bridge between academic learning and industry needs.

🔭

Our Vision

To become India's largest and most trusted platform for student-developer collaboration, creating a ecosystem where every student can build a professional portfolio while earning, and every business can find the perfect talent for their projects.

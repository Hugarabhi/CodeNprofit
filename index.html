The Current Market Reality

In India, there is a huge disconnect between businesses and student talent. Even though both exist in large numbers, they rarely connect directly. Let's break the wall!!

Problems Faced by Businesses

  • No Professional Website Many MSMEs remain offline despite strong products.
  • Weak Social Media & Marketing Lack of digital marketing and online representation.
  • High Agency Costs Difficulty competing with digitally active competitors due to expensive dev fees.
  • No Creative Talent Access Business owners are busy; no access to creative developers.

Problems Faced by Students

  • No Real-World Experience Graduating with only college assignments and no real clients.
  • Weak Professional Portfolio Difficulty building an industry-level portfolio.
  • Limited Earning Opportunities Lack of paid work or "pocket money" while learning.
  • The Education-Industry Gap Still no perfect soft skills or understanding of real business operations.

How CodenProfit Works

Creating a transparent, collaborative, and opportunity-driven B2S ecosystem.

01

Business Posts Project

Business owner submits their idea, requirement (website/app), budget, and timeline expectations.

02

Project Gets Listed

Project appears on the platform. Verified student developers view and apply for the opportunity.

03

Students Build Solution

Students analyze, design, and develop. Business provides feedback and feature requests during development.

04

Mentor Quality Check

Crucial step: A mentor/PM rechecks implementation, verifies security, and ensures no technical mismatches.

05

Business Receives Product

After verification, the approved project is delivered. The business launches and uses the digital solution.

06

Students Gain Rewards

Students earn fair payment while learning, gain real industry exposure, and build a strong professional portfolio.

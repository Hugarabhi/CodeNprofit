Learn how to navigate the platform, find projects, hire developers, and make the most of your experience.

Welcome to CodenProfit!

India's first Business-to-Student (B2S) platform connecting talented student developers with innovative companies. Whether you're here to build your portfolio or find the perfect developer for your project, we've got you covered.

For Developers

Getting Started as a Developer

01

Create Your Account

Sign up as a developer using your email or Google account. Choose "Developer" as your role and fill in your basic information.

Use your college email for verification
02

Complete Your Profile

Add your skills, experience, GitHub portfolio, and set your minimum project budget. A complete profile gets 4x more hire requests!

Add at least 5 skills to appear in search
03

Browse Projects

Explore available projects that match your skills. You can see project titles, categories, and budgets without a subscription.

Use filters to find relevant projects
04

Subscribe for Premium

Projects above ₹1000 require a premium subscription (₹69/month) to view full details and apply. Projects under ₹1000 are completely free!

Start with free projects to build your portfolio
05

Apply to Projects

Once subscribed, you can view full project details including host contact information and apply directly with a personalized message.

Customize each application for better results
06

Get Hired & Deliver

If selected, you'll receive a hire request. Accept it, communicate with the host, deliver your work, and get paid!

Keep all communication on the platform

For Companies/Hosts

Getting Started as a Host

01

Create Company Account

Sign up as a host with your company details. Provide your company name, industry, and team size to build trust with developers.

Verified companies get more applications
02

Post Your First Project

Fill in project details: title, description, required skills, budget range, and timeline. Be specific to attract the right developers.

Clear requirements = better proposals
03

Review Applications

Developers will apply to your project. Review their profiles, skills, and previous work. You can accept or reject applications.

Check their portfolio and GitHub
04

Send Hire Requests

Found a developer you like? Send them a direct hire request with a personalized message about your project.

Mention why you chose them specifically
05

Manage Payments

Payments are held securely until project completion. Release payment only when you're satisfied with the delivered work.

Use milestone payments for large projects
06

Leave Reviews

After project completion, leave a review for the developer. This helps build the community and rewards quality work.

Be honest and constructive

Understanding Premium Subscription

₹69/month – Unlock the full potential

What you get:

  • View full project details & requirements
  • See host contact information & phone numbers
  • Apply to all projects directly
  • Priority placement in search results
  • Exclusive opportunities from top companies

How to subscribe:

  1. Go to Browse Projects page
  2. Click "Get Subscription" button
  3. Choose payment method (UPI, GPay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  4. Complete payment of ₹69
  5. Submit payment screenshot for verification
  6. Receive coupon code via email within 24 hours

Note: Projects under ₹1000 are always free – no subscription needed!

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started as a developer?
Sign up, complete your profile with skills and portfolio, browse free projects, and subscribe for premium access to unlock all opportunities.
How do I post a project as a company?
Create a host account, go to "My Projects", click "Post New Project", fill in the details, and publish. Developers will start applying!
What is the premium subscription?
₹69/month gives you access to all premium projects (above ₹1000) with full contact details and unlimited applications.
How do payments work?
Payments are held securely and released to developers only after host approval. Both parties are protected.
What are skill exams?
Take exams to prove your expertise. Each passed exam earns you a star on your profile. Pass all 5 to become a Top Developer!
How do I refer friends?
Use your unique referral link from the Referral card. When 10 friends join, you get a free Professional Gift Combo!
Can I collaborate with other developers?
Yes! The "Connect Developer" feature lets you assign tasks to other developers and split project earnings.
What if I need help?
Contact us at Support@codenprofit.com or use the contact form. We're here 24/7 to assist you!
